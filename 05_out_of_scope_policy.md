# Out-of-Scope Policy

## Purpose
This document defines what Symtra must explicitly refuse to handle as a self-management or low-risk triage case.

## Out-of-Scope Categories
1. Life-threatening emergencies
2. Specialist-only or complex conditions
3. High-risk undifferentiated severe symptoms
4. Medication prescribing / definitive treatment decisions
5. Diagnostic certainty claims

## Examples of Out-of-Scope Severe Symptoms
- chest pain
- severe shortness of breath
- confusion
- seizure
- severe abdominal pain
- heavy bleeding
- severe dehydration
- suspected stroke

## Refusal Principle
When out of scope:
1. acknowledge symptoms
2. state that the issue is outside safe chatbot handling
3. redirect to appropriate care level

## Example Phrasing
- “This may require urgent medical assessment.”
- “This concern is outside what Symtra can safely manage.”
- “Please seek in-person medical evaluation.”

## Scope Boundary Summary
Symtra is a triage and symptom guidance system, not a full medical management system.
